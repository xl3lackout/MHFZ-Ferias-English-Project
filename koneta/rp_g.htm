GRP
  • If your HR is 999 and one weapon is SR999, you can accept the guild request quest, and you can become GR by clearing it.
  • The reward money (Gz) for G-rank quests is not divided by the number of participants in the quest.
  • In addition to the hunter who accepted the quest, the contract money will also be taken from the participating hunters as well.
    ※The contract fee will not be returned if the quest is completed.
  • There is no reduction of GRP and Gz due to the number of times a person faints during a quest.
    ※It will be returned when you retire from the quest.
  • With GR1, Gooks will be upgraded to G-rank. (addition of digging)
  • With GR50, My Tore Poogie will be upgraded to G rank. (Can be equipped with G skill cuff)
  • With GR100, My Tore Worker Cats will be upgraded to G-rank. (G-rank adventure destination added)
  • Halk will be upgraded to G rank at GR150. (Can be equipped with 3 skill books, adds G-rank books)
  • At GR200, high-rank sword crystals will be sold.
  • At GR350, you can give a G-class Poogie one item. During the quest, there is a certain probability that you will use the items you have.

GSR

  • SR999's book of secrets can be upgraded to a G-rank book of secrets.
  • If your GSR is 100 or higher, you will be able to set two secret book effects.
  • GSRP is twice as much as GRP.
  • For G-rank hardcore quests, GRP is doubled.
