Sigils

Up to three special effects can be attached to each sigil, and up to three can be put onto a G-rank weapon.
To remove a sigil、「Sigil Catalyst」is required.
For special effects, the effect of the recipe is prioritized at the time of production, and if it is off or unpredictable, it will be random.
