Partner

You can register at the "Rasta Tavern" which can be entered from the entry zone in Mezeporta Square.
Accept the quest and bring back materials.
Requirements
　1.HR2+
　2.Purchased House Expansion 1

Registering a Partner
Talk to a Legendary Rasta and select "Partner" to start partner registration.
The weapon type of the Legend Rasta you spoke to at that time becomes the partner's initial weapon type.
　(Other weapon types can be acquired by talking to the corresponding Legend Rasta for every PR10.)
Once you have selected your gender and personality, your partners appearance will be selected in your House.
You can change your partners appearance at the beauty salon in the plaza, just like a hunter.

NPC accompany priority
Legendary＞Partner＞Temporary Rasta＞Normal Rasta＞Fosta

Partner Equipment
The hunter can "gift" equipment to the partner so that they can equip it.
My Set registration, decos, and Sigils on equipment must be removed before they can be gifted.
　(The gifted equipment will disappear from the hunter's Equipment box and will be stored in the partner's exclusive equipment box.)
Even after the gift is given, equipment can be upgraded by selecting the partner at the blacksmith.

Armor
・Defense is the same for each type regardless of LV
・No skills or special effects
Weapons
・Weapon power is the same for each type
・Element value is 0, status attacks such as para or sleep are transferred
　(The abnormal status value is halved for tonfa)
・No special effects

Partner Growth
By having them accompany you on quests, your PR (partner rank) and "weapon proficiency" will increase.
PR leveling is the same as HR. Obtained PRP calculation is as follows
Normal Quest
・HRP obtained×0.4=PRP
・HRP obtained×0.2=Weapon proficiency
G-rank quest
・GRP obtained×1.2=PRP
・GRP obtained×0.7=Weapon proficiency
When the proficiency of one weapon is at 999, the weapon multiplier gains a +5 bonus, which is also applied to other weapons.
